FRIDA: A Collaborative Robot Painter

Peter Schaldenbrand, James McCann, & Jean Oh
The Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University

FRIDA, a Framework and Robotics Initiative for Developing Arts, is an ongoing robot painting and creative artificial intelligence project at CMU developed by PhD student Peter Schaldenbrand and principal investigators James McCann and Jean Oh

FRIDA can paint from multiple modes of inspiration from the artist in collaboration: reference images and language descripions in different styles given by example images. Composition can be influenced by a user drawn rough sketch. FRIDA is open source and evolving.
