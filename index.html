I am a PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Institute advised by Dr. Jean Oh. My research goal is to lower the barriers that people face in performing acts of creativity such as painting, composing music, or creating video content by supporting creative expression with artificial intelligence. In particular, I am creating AI tools that generate visual content using elementary skills such as language and decisions as input. Such tools can enable people with a lack of mobility or cognitive impairments to engage in creative expression and art therapy using the skills and abilities that they do have.
